Overview

I had an opportunity that works as a UX design apprentice at BRIDGEGOOD. BRIDGEGOOD is a non-profit organization that supports design students develop their skills and industry knowledge. My teammates and I were assigned a design sprint to create the internship/entry-level job opportunities feature to provide inclusive and accessible job opportunities for entry-level design students. During work with my tames, I had a chance to do a product lead & verious users interview, wireframe, and prototype.

Problem

Job hunting is not easy for students since they lack experience. However, most companies prefer applicants with more than two years of experience, and they post many requirements on the job posting. It makes students overwhelmed and hesitant to apply. Students from the BRIDGEGOOD UX Design Apprenticeship have limited-to-no industry experience and want to gain real-world experience by getting hired for an internship.

Design Challenge

Designing an Internship/Entry Level Job Opportunities feature on BRIDGEGOOD.com, where companies can post available internship positions for students to apply to. BRIDGEGOOD can receive referral fees by connecting qualified students to companies.

2) Competitor Audit

I had a responsibility to analyze the competitors. We choose the most popular job posting platform and I took a charge to analizing those job platforms.

Strength

  • Social media platform
  • Simple to look for job and filtering
  • The most popular job searching platform
  • Strong content and feartures for job seeker

Weakness

  • May create a unverified or fake profile
  • Privacy
  • Premium membership
  • Intense competition (e.g. Facebook)

Strength

  • University partnership
  • Partnership with out of state university (UK)
  • Exclusive career fair

Weakness

  • Lack of community college partnership
  • Not every major university or college is on the platform
  • Platform fee

Strength

  • Helping start-ups get connect to both investor and employees
  • More visualized job description (Company Photos & Videos)
  • Using keywords on job description

Weakness

  • Lack of contents for job seeker
  • Confuse to navigate website
  • May create a unverified or fake profile
