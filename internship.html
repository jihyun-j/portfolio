Overview

I had the opportunity to work as a UX design apprentice at BRIDGEGOOD. BRIDGEGOOD is a non-profit organization that supports design students develop skills and help them gain industry knowledge. My teammates and I worked on creating a feature that highlighted internship / entry-level job opportunities. The goal was to make opportunities both inclusive and accessible for students seeking entry-level design positions.

Problem

Job hunting is not easy for students since they lack experience. Most companies prefer applicants with more than two years of experience, and there are many requirements on the job posting. This is overwhelming for most students, and explains why a lot of them are hesitant to apply.

Design Challenge

Designing an Internship/Entry Level Job Opportunities feature on BRIDGEGOOD.com, where companies can post available internship positions for students to apply to. BRIDGEGOOD can receive referral fees by connecting qualified students to companies.

Competitor Audit

To better understand what makes a job posting platform great, I conducted research into the top job posting platforms for students. Below are the strengths and weaknesses of each job platform based on my research.

Strength

  • Social media platform
  • Simple to look for job and filtering
  • The most popular job searching platform
  • Strong content and feartures for job seeker

Weakness

  • May create a unverified or fake profile
  • Privacy
  • Premium membership
  • Intense competition (e.g. Facebook)

Strength

  • University partnership
  • Partnership with out of state university (UK)
  • Exclusive career fair

Weakness

  • Lack of community college partnership
  • Not every major university or college is on the platform
  • Platform fee

Strength

  • Helping start-ups get connect to both investor and employees
  • More visualized job description (Company Photos & Videos)
  • Using keywords on job description

Weakness

  • Lack of contents for job seeker
  • Confuse to navigate website
  • May create a unverified or fake profile

The Solution

Partnership with potential employers.

Validate that the companies are from the design or technology industry.

Easy to search job opportunities.

Students can search based on specialty, type of role and location. We also provide a helpful message to encourage students to apply.

Non-traditional applications.

Simplify the application process by removing education and cover letter requirements.

Tracking job application.

BRIDGEGOOD can keep track of students that got hired and provide referrals to prospective students. It can also show the application status whether its pending, accepted or rejected to inform students with updates.
