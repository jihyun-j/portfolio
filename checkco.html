Overview

This is my passion project that taught me the fundamentals and process of UX design. The inspiration for my project was based on a documentary called ‚ÄòToxic Beauty‚Äô that showcased how harmful cosmetics and personal care products can be to the human body. I designed the Checkco app in a way that was easy to use, and helpful in checking the ingredients of cosmetic and personal care products for those that wanted to avoid harmful chemicals and be conscious of what they were using on their bodies.

Problem

We use the same products in the shower yet have no idea what the active ingredients are. People regularly use skincare products without knowing whether or not the product they are using is safe to use. Furthermore, the listed ingredients are mixed with chemical jargon making it difficult for the average person to understand without any explanations.

Design Challenge

How can we design an app that makes it easy to understand the ingredients used in our most loved skincare products?

The Process

Research

Initial Questions

  • How do users purchase cosmetic products?
  • What are the most important factors when purchasing cosmetic products?
  • Do users care about ingredients?

These are the steps (both qualitative and quantitative) in my research process:

  1. Sent surveys to randomly selected users that asked them the initial questions.
  2. Conducted in-depth user interviews with users in the target audience and learned their pain points and needs.
  3. Created affinity mapping for the group and organized the information.

The Solution

Sign-up & Profile

Create your skin profile

When users sign up for the app. They will be prompted to list information regarding their skin type and preferences. Based on this information, the app will provide recommendations on skincare products that work best for their skin type in the Home Screen.

Search

Easy to search products / ingredients

The app has a feature that allows an user to scan the product‚Äôs barcode to retrieve the list of ingredients and highlight harmful chemicals. It also provides users with recommended products for easy and safe discovery of products that work for their skin type.

Product Details

Check the Safety of Skin Care Products

Using colors to visualize and highlight the safety rating for a given skin care product.

Compare

Choosing the best skin care product for your skin

Users often spend a lot of time comparing different products. The app features a compare option that provides the safety rating for similar products, providing the user with a quick and easy way to compare different brands.
